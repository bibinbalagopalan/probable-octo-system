## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 14 - Nov - 2025

### Introduction

At Jeevadhara, we believe in complete transparency and respect for your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our educational platform.

<div class="static-highlight-box">
<h3>Our Fundamental Principle</h3>
<p>We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. Your privacy is protected by design.</p>
</div>

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely clear about what we don't collect:

<div class="static-highlight-box">
<h3>No Personal Data Collection</h3>
<ul class="static-list">
<li><strong>No registration required:</strong> You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information</li>
<li><strong>No tracking:</strong> We don't use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies</li>
<li><strong>No personal identifiers:</strong> We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information</li>
<li><strong>No usage data:</strong> We don't monitor or store how you use our platform</li>
<li><strong>No location data:</strong> We don't collect your IP address or geographical location</li>
<li><strong>No device information:</strong> We don't collect data about your device, browser, or operating system</li>
</ul>
</div>

## How Our Platform Works

### Accessing Content

You can freely access all our educational materials—notes, quizzes, and study resources—without providing any personal information. There are no login requirements, no forms to fill out, and no data collection of any kind.

### Browsing Anonymously

When you visit Jeevadhara, you browse completely anonymously. We don't have the technical capability to identify who you are or track your activities on our platform.

<div class="static-notice-box">
<h3>Completely Anonymous Learning</h3>
<p>Learn with complete peace of mind. Read our content, take quizzes, and use our resources without worrying about your privacy. We've designed our platform to be accessible without compromising your personal information.</p>
</div>

## Third-Party Services

### No External Integrations

Our platform does not integrate with any third-party services that might collect data on our behalf. This means:

- No social media plugins
- No advertising networks
- No analytics services
- No external tracking tools

### Hosting Infrastructure

While our website is hosted on servers that technically process requests, we do not log or store any identifiable information from these technical processes.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, you don't need to worry about:

- Requesting access to your personal data
- Asking for data correction
- Requesting data deletion
- Objecting to data processing
- Data portability requests

<div class="static-notice-box">
<h3>No Action Required</h3>
<p>You don't need to do anything to protect your privacy on our platform. We've built privacy protection into the very foundation of Jeevadhara.</p>
</div>

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy that involve collecting data, we will:

1. Clearly notify users through prominent announcements
2. Explain exactly what data we plan to collect and why
3. Provide opt-out options where possible
4. Maintain our commitment to transparency

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, you can reach out through our website's contact section.

<div class="static-highlight-box">
<h3>Our Promise</h3>
<p>We believe education should be free and private. We will never monetize your data because we don't collect it in the first place. Our mission is education, not data collection.</p>
</div>

## Legal Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with major privacy regulations including:

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
