## Why Your Support Matters

Jeevadhara was born from a simple belief: quality education should be free for all. While our volunteers provide their expertise without compensation, running our platform involves essential costs that we need your help to cover.

<div class="static-highlight-box">
<h3>Where Your Contribution Goes</h3>
<ul class="static-list">
<li><strong>Platform Maintenance:</strong> Keeping our website running smoothly and securely</li>
<li><strong>Content Development:</strong> Creating new study materials and updating existing content</li>
<li><strong>Server Costs:</strong> Ensuring our resources are available to students 24/7</li>
<li><strong>Outreach Programs:</strong> Reaching students in remote and underserved areas</li>
<li><strong>Technology Upgrades:</strong> Improving the learning experience for everyone</li>
</ul>
</div>
